Hi, I’m Yana!

Travels have always been my dream, and through this blog, I combine my love for exploring new places with my passion for design and storytelling.

On my travels, I’m never alone. There’s always a pair of long ears peeking out of my backpack — yes, you guessed it — my cute handmade bunnies. Together, we visit beautiful countries, discover fun facts, and taste local dishes. These little adventures always bring me so much joy and inspiration.

The bunnies are perfect travel companions — always ready for new experiences and tasty treats!

How did it start?

But why bunnies, and how did this idea begin? The answer is simple: I love bunnies, and my mom loves crocheting.

During the quiet days of the pandemic, crocheting became a calming and inspiring hobby for her. At first, she made simple, funny bunny toys, and I started taking cute photos of them everywhere.

Then, one day, she created the first bunny couple in Ukrainian style. I took them on vacation and snapped some photos to show my mom that the bunnies were also having a good time with me. That was the beginning of our journey.

How is it going now?

The idea of making bunnies in different styles inspired by different countries was so adorable — and maybe a little crazy, but nothing could stop my mom’s passion for crocheting.

Over the past two years, our bunny family has grown to include German, Greek, Spanish, Italian, Swiss, Danish, and French couples. Now, wherever I travel, I take these little companions along to capture special memories — and to share them with my mom… and with you! Our bunny family
